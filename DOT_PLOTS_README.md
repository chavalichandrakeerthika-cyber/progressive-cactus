## INTERPETING THE DOT PLOTS  
<img width="515" height="136" alt="dotplots" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b7019282-04b1-4e5b-93ec-33f91227f049" />  

Dot plots were made between extant descendants and their immediate ancestral node's reconstructed genome from progressive cactus. They are in the form of HTML webpages, can be downloaded (2-4MB) and viewed on browser.   

Files are named as <extant>_<ancestor>_dotplot.html  

As a "control", a dot plot between two closely related extant (sister) species was also generated: Bric_Bede_dotplot.html  
(This plot had less noise and was muhc cleaner than the ones with the ancestors).
